The basic documents required include:

  • Identity Proof (PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID)

Our loan approval process is quick and efficient. Once you submit all required documents, the approval typically takes 24-48 hours. After approval, the loan amount is disbursed within 24 hours directly to your bank account.
